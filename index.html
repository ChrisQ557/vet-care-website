Dog Care

Dog Care Details

Our veterinary team offers expert guidance and services to ensure your dog stays healthy, clean, and happy. Below are some of the key areas we focus on:

Vaccination is one of the most important ways to protect your dog from contagious and potentially life-threatening diseases. We offer both core vaccines—such as rabies, parvovirus, distemper, and adenovirus—and non-core vaccines depending on lifestyle, such as leptospirosis or kennel cough. Puppies require a series of shots, while adult dogs need regular boosters to maintain immunity.

Regular grooming isn’t just for looks—it’s essential for your dog’s health. Our grooming services include coat brushing, trimming, bathing with dog-safe shampoos, nail clipping, and ear cleaning. We tailor every session based on your dog’s breed, coat type, and skin sensitivity to keep them clean, comfortable, and looking their best.

Dental health affects your dog’s entire body. Plaque buildup can lead to gum disease, tooth loss, and even organ damage if bacteria enter the bloodstream. We offer thorough dental checkups, cleanings under safe anesthesia, and advice on at-home brushing and dental treats. Prevention is key to avoiding painful issues down the line.

Every dog is different, and their diet should be too. We create customized nutrition plans based on breed, age, weight, activity level, and health conditions like allergies or kidney issues. Whether you feed kibble, wet food, or a raw diet, we guide you to make safe, balanced, and effective choices to support your dog’s long-term wellness.

Routine checkups help us spot changes in your dog’s health before they become serious. We monitor weight, behavior, heart and lung function, joint mobility, and more during annual or semi-annual exams. Our records track your dog’s health over time, ensuring early detection of illnesses and timely treatment when needed.

Cat Care

Cat Care Details

Cats have unique needs when it comes to veterinary care. From preventative treatments to daily wellbeing, our experienced team provides personalized support tailored for feline health and comfort.

We protect cats against key diseases such as feline panleukopenia, calicivirus, herpesvirus, and rabies. Vaccination schedules vary based on age, lifestyle (indoor vs. outdoor), and medical history.

While cats are natural groomers, they still benefit from professional grooming to manage shedding, prevent matting, and reduce hairballs—especially for long-haired breeds.

Dental disease is common in cats and can lead to pain, tooth loss, or systemic issues. We provide thorough cleanings and show you how to care for your cat's teeth at home.

Cats are obligate carnivores and need a high-protein diet. We guide you on quality food choices and ways to keep your cat hydrated—especially important for those prone to urinary tract issues.

Annual or biannual exams help detect early signs of illness, monitor weight, and discuss behavior or age-related concerns—ensuring a longer, healthier life for your feline friend.
